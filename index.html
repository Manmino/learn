Lesson 1

I want.

A wan.

You want.

Ni wan.

I eat.

A sik

You eat.

Ni sik./

I want it.

A ko wan ( I want this). A co wan (I want that). A yo wan (I want that yonder).

Do you want it?

Ni ko/co/yo wan (ka)?

Yes.

Bana (Correct).

Why?

Ha yu?

Because I want to eat it.

Yu a ko/co sik wan.

Lesson 2

Did you eat it?

Ni ko/co sik le (ka)?

Yes, I ate it.

Bana, a ko/co sik le.

That is good. Now what does he want?

(Co) haw ya. Gim, ka hakka (lu) wan (ka)?

He wants to drink.

Ka mwisu wan (He wants water).

Can he drink?

Ka mwisu sik neng (ka)? (Can he consume water?)

He can drink.

Ka neng.

Can you drink? Can I drink?

Ni mwisu sik neng? A mwisu sik neng?

I can drink and you can drink.

A mwisu sik neng i ni mwisu sik neng.
